Why to choose Englaland School?

We have classes with native speakers

You've probably noticed that people who leave abroad and communicate with foreigners, they learn English many times faster, because they plunge into the English speaking environment. At Englaland, you can choose a teacher who is a native speaker.

Meetings at the language club

There is a language club every Friday at the school. This is a special format of classes in which you can communicate with both native speakers and other students, watch original films or play games. The main condition is that everything is in English only.

Cambridge Methodology

In addition to the fact that you immerse yourself in the language environment as much as possible when learning a language, during the training the teacher uses all 6 senses. It also increases the memorability of the material at times.

Super-profitable promotions and gifts

Englaland School regularly runs promotions, thanks to which you can get 1-2 months of language learning for free. And this is not only a result, but also a real saving of money!
